Our Story & Mission


We believe in the power of books to uplift, educate, and transform lives. WeDonateBooks began as an effort by high schoolers in the suburbs of Minnesota to spread a love of books throughout their school and community. Since then, through book drive + swap events, we have amassesed nearly 1000 books that we plan to donate to organizations that need them most.

How We Work

  • We collect gently used books through local drives and community swaps.
  • We sort and catalog them by genre and condition.
  • We partner with organizations to schedule drop-offs or pickups based on their needs.

Our Values

  • Devotion: We work tirelessly to ensure that everyone in our community who wants access to books can get them.
  • Access: We believe books should be available to everyone, regardless of where they come from or their financial abilities.
  • Community: We work together to harness the resources and power of everyday individuals to strengthen our communities.
